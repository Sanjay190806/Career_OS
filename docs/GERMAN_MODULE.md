# German Module

The German module is available at `/german` and from the sidebar label `German`.

## Lesson Flow

- Lesson 1 is unlocked by default.
- Lesson 2 unlocks after Lesson 1 is complete.
- Lesson N unlocks after Lesson N-1 is complete.
- Completed lessons show as done and can be reopened for review.
- Locked lessons show `Complete previous lesson to unlock this.`

## Lesson Drawer

Each lesson opens into a drawer with:

- title, level, objective, estimated time, and XP reward
- vocabulary
- grammar
- examples
- mini quiz
- notes
- Ask Shayla actions
- Complete Lesson

## Progress

Completing lessons updates German XP, streak, level, completed lesson count, and achievement checks through the career store.

## Shayla Integration

German lesson buttons send lesson-specific context to Shayla AI Mentor. No AI call happens automatically on page load.
